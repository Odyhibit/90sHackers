What is LeetSpeak?

Leet (or "1337"), also known as eleet or leetspeak, is a system of modified spellings used primarily on the Internet. It often uses character replacements in ways that play on the similarity of their glyphs via reflection or other resemblance.

Leet speak is a way of speaking that was born in the early days of the internet. It was used by hackers to prevent their websites from being found by search engines. It was also used by gamers to communicate with each other in a way that was difficult for outsiders to understand.
